Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Resume and professional information
  • Job application history

Usage Information

  • Service interaction data
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookie and tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Train and enhance our AI models
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. AI Processing

Our AI-powered features:

  • Process your content to provide recommendations
  • Use anonymized data for model improvement
  • Do not share personal information with third parties
  • Allow you to opt-out of AI improvement contributions

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure data centers

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Analytics partners (in anonymized form)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of certain data processing

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage
  • Personalize content
  • Improve our services

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@jobgenie.com
